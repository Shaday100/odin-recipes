Chewy Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

As a self-proclaimed cookie enthusiast, this recipe is my absolute favorite go-to for a quick dessert treat! What's not to love? This delicious treat has just about everything I'd want in a cookie- they're soft, chewy, and best of all, easy to make! Most of these ingredients can be found easily in the kitchen, and the cookie dough is freezable if the portion turns out to be a tad excessive.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened>
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • (optional) 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter and both sugars in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time until well combined; stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; stir into the creamed sugar mixture until well combined.
  4. Mix in quick oats, chocolate chips, and (if using) chopped walnuts. Drop batter by heaping spoonfuls onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until bottoms are starting to brown. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for five minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
