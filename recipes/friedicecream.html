Fried Ice Cream

Description

With a crispy exterior and a smooth and creamy exterior, this treat is sure to be one you'll enjoy! Feel free to add toppings on the finished product. I personally am a huge fan of caramel drizzle and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups crushed cornflakes cereal
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 quart ice cream of your choice
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 3 egg whites

Steps

  1. Scoop icecream into eight 1/2 cup-sized balls. Place on a baking sheet and freeze for about one hour, or until firm.
  2. Combine crushed cornflakes and cinnamon in a shallow dish. Beat egg whites until foamy in a separate bowl.
  3. Roll icecream balls in egg whites, then in cornflakes, covering ice cream completely. Freeze again until firm, about 3 hours.
  4. Heat oil in deep fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Fry ice cream balls 1 or 2 at a time in heated oil until golden, about 10 to 15 seconds. Drain quickly on paper towels and serve immediately.
